Algorand Foundation Ecosystem Support Program

The Algorand Foundation is launching a 200 million Algo Ecosystem Support Program. This broad, multi-year Program will fund research, development, education, and community engagement, supporting the growth of a vibrant Algorand ecosystem.

Research Proposals

We support research on a wide spectrum of domains pertaining to blockchains, cryptocurrencies, and distributed systems, and more generally computer science, governance, and economics.

Development Tools

We will fund the creation of tools to support and promote development over the Algorand blockchain, forming the foundation on which the network and ecosystem can grow.

Applications and Use Cases

We will fund building and deployment of dApps and use cases on Algorand that take advantage of its superior functionalities.
